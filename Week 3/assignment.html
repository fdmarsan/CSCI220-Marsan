Experiment of the Effects of Caffeine on GPA

Conducted by Jennifer (who works in a lab)

This experiment seeks to determine if a correlation exists between the amount of caffeine an individual consumes daily and that individual's grade point average in school.

100 students were assigned numbers from 1-100 and were chosen at random into three groups:

- Group A was given caffeine equivalent to the national average (3 cups) - Group B was given twice the amount of the national daily average (6 cups) - Group C was the control group and therefore were given a placebo
